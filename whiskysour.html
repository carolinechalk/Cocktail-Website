Whisky Sour

Equipment
  • A shaker
  • A strainer
  • Short glass
Ingredients
  • 2 parts bourbon
  • 1 part lemon juice
  • ½ part sugar syrup
  • ½ part egg white
  • Cubed ice
  • 1 cherry and an orange slice
